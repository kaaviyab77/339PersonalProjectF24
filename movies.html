Welcome to my favorite movies!

Here are some of my favorite movies. These movies are movies that I watched recently and very much enjoyed. I recommend watching these movies! I will include a short summary of each movie as well as link to a trailer to each movie.

Bahubali The Beginning - 2015

This movie was one of the biggest, if not the biggest, Indian movies ever made. This movie was officially released in 4 different languages and was an expierence to watch. It was one of the first indian movies that felt comparable to a Hollywood movie that I have seen in awhile. And the story and twists of the movie just keeps you watching. This movie had a cast of very strong actors and the settings where breath taking and only sucked you in further into the movie. This movie is only the first of the 2 part series.


Summary from imdb:
A child from the Mahishmati kingdom is raised by tribal people and one day learns about his royal heritage, his father's bravery in battle and a mission to overthrow the incumbent ruler.

Chandramukhi - 2005

This movie came out almost 2 decades ago, yet it is one of my all time favorite movies. I did not fully watch this movie until I was 13 or so, and it instatnly become one of my favorites. This movie is not beloved by me because of its horror elements, but rather its comedy. The plot is pretty standard, and the horror effects, is not very scary, but the comedy carries this entire movie and will keep you laughing at the same jokes even decades later.


Summary from imdb:
Creepy happenings in an abandoned mansion are attributed to the ghost of an ancient courtesan, back for revenge.

Deadpool & Wolvarine - 2024

This movie is the most recent movie I have watched. Although this is not my favorite movie, not even my favorite Deadpool movie, but it is a fun watch nonetheless.


Summary from imdb:
Deadpool is offered a place in the Marvel Cinematic Universe by the Time Variance Authority, but instead recruits a variant of Wolverine to save his universe from extinction.

Detective Chinatown - 2015

This is a movie that I thoroughly enjoyed and highly recommend watching. This is a comedy action film that is filled with a lot of hilarious gags and a very captivating mystery at the center of it.


Summary from imdb:
After being rejected from the police academy, a mild-mannered young man travels to Bangkok where he and an energetic distant relative must solve a murder case.

Jil Jung Juk - 2016

This is another really funny movie, in which 3 men go through twists and turns in order to smuggle drugs without getting caught.


Summary from imdb:
In 2020, three young blokes, Jil, Jung and Juck, with nothing in common between them, are assigned the task of transporting an Ambassador car coated with cocaine-paint by the dreaded drug lord, Dheivanayagam. Along the way, they encounter Narasimhan with an almost identical car, who makes small talk and tries to gel with the trio. After a night of drinking the trio escape the pub without his knowledge. But only later do they realize that in a drunken stupor, they have taken Narasimhan's car and not their own.

Kolamavu Kokila - 2018

This is a movie that I was only able to watch recently despite it being out for awhile. This is because this movie is really hard to find for some reason. If you are able to I would highly recommend watching this movie. IT has very good music and is another funny movie. It also shows a female character as being mentally strong and very smart. It overall is just an amazong watch.


Summary from imdb:
To save her mother from terminal cancer, a young girl gets herself involved with the drug trafficking mafia active in the small towns of Tamil Nadu.

The Spongebob Squarepants Movie - 2004

This movie is simply a fun watch and just a great movie to watch to unwind.


Summary from imdb:
After King Neptune's crown is stolen, SpongeBob and Patrick go on a quest in 6 days to retrieve his crown. On the way SpongeBob and Patrick defeat many evildoers using their brains and bronzes. While this is happening someone is taking over Bikini Bottom and SpongeBob and Patrick must defeat this mastermind.

The Bob's Burgers Movie - 2022

If ypou like Bob's Burgers, you'll like this movie. The animation quality is amazing, and there is also a very intriguing mystery that takes place.


Summary from imdb:
The story begins when a ruptured water main creates an enormous sinkhole right in front of Bob's Burgers, blocking the entrance indefinitely and ruining the Belchers' plans for a successful summer. While Bob and Linda struggle to keep the business afloat, the kids try to solve a mystery that could save their family's restaurant. As the dangers mount, these underdogs help each other find hope and fight to get back behind the counter, where they belong.

The Croods - 2013

This has to be one of my favorite DreamWorks films. It is funny, wholesome, colorful, and creative. I rewatched this movie many many times, and it continues to be an enjoyable movie, and one that I never get bored of.


Summary from imdb:
"The Croods" are an eccentric family of cavemen, who survive the harsh terrain by living accordingly to a strict set of rules. But when their home is destroyed in the wake of an impending disaster known as "The End", they are forced to leave their home of shelter and security, and into the wilderness of the unknown to find a new home

Varuthapadatha Valibar Sangam - 2013

This is a halirous movie that is just a slice of life comedy, and is great when you want to just watch a light hearted movie.


Summary from imdb:
Set in a village called Silukkuvarpatti in Tamil Nadu, the story is about a group of wastrels lead by Bosepandi (Sivakarthikeyan) and his loyal sidekick Soori. Bosepandi is attracted to Kalyani (Bindu Madhavi), a school teacher. He seeks the help of Lathapandi (Sri Divya), a class 12 student in the same school, to deliver love letters from him to Kalyani. As time goes by, Kalyani gets married to someone, leaving Bosepandi heartbroken. Meanwhile, Lathapandi's father arranges for her wedding, but she is still a minor. When Bosepandi comes to learn about the marriage, he seeks the help of police to stop the teen marriage. When the wedding is called off, a relieved Latha falls for Bose. What happens between Latha and Bose? This forms the rest of the story of the film.
