Welcome to my favorite shows!

Here are some of my favorite shows. These movies are movies that I watched recently and very much enjoyed. I recommend watching these movies! I will include a short summary of each movie as well as link to a trailer to each movie.

American Dad - 2005-Present

This is a series that I discovered pretty recently and I absolutely love binging this show. Although at times this show has some conservitive views that I don't agree with, it is still an enjoyable watch.


Summary from imdb:
The escapades of Stan Smith, a conservative C.I.A. Agent dealing with family life, and keeping America safe.

Overlord - 2015-2022

This is technically an on going series with a movie that very recently came out. This show is very interesting and funny. It is fun to watch a normal man trapped inside an undead body being revered by overpowered characters and try to navigate a completely new world with a very different system.


Summary from imdb:
The final hour of the popular virtual reality game Yggdrasil has come. However, Momonga, a powerful wizard and master of the dark guild Ainz Ooal Gown, decides to spend his last few moments in the game as the servers begin to shut down. To his surprise, despite the clock having struck midnight, Momonga is still fully conscious as his character and, moreover, the non-player characters appear to have developed personalities of their own. Confronted with this abnormal situation, Momonga commands his loyal servants to help him investigate and take control of this new world, with the hopes of figuring out what has caused this development and if there may be others in the same predicament.

Russian Doll - 2019

This is a show based around a time loop and it is very intriguing and really takes a look at the character Nadia, and how she reevaluates her life. This is a overall very serious series with very light comedy, but still a very enjoyable watch.


Summary from imdb:
On Nadia's 36th birthday she is struck by a car and killed while leaving her party. In an instant she is alive again and transported back to her birthday party earlier that night. Moments later she dies again and finds herself, once again, back at her party, Nadia begins to question her sanity as she strives to unravel the mystery of her situation.

So I'm A Spider, So What - 2021

This show is very interesting, it jumps back and forth in the timeline and kinda shows the true nature of people and how they would act when given too much power. It also questions how much athourity a teacher should have, and in the name of prolonging someone's life, is it okay to imprision them? The main character is fun and inspiring and I highly recommend watching this anime.


Summary from imdb:
The world where the Hero and the Demon King continue to oppose each other. The grand magic from the Hero and the Demon King cross over worlds and exploded in the classroom of a certain high school. The students who died in the explosion will be reincarnated in a different world. Kumoko who has the lowest reputation in the class, was reincarnated into the form of a spider. Nevertheless, she adapted quickly to the present condition with strong willpower. This is a story of she who has become, a spider trying whatever she can to live.

Spongebob Squarepants - 1999-Present

I feel like this show is very self explainitory.


Summary from imdb:
The character-driven cartoon chronicles the nautical and often nonsensical adventures of SpongeBob, an incurably optimistic and earnest sea sponge, and his underwater friends. Dwelling a few fathoms beneath the tropical isle of Bikini Atoll in the sub-surface city of Bikini Bottom, SpongeBob lives in a two-story pineapple. Instead of taking the logical approach to everyday challenges, SpongeBob approaches life in a wayward and unconventional way. Whether searching for the ultimate spatula to perfect his burger flipping technique at the Krusty Krab or just hanging out with his best friend Patrick (an amiable starfish), SpongeBob's good intentions and overzealous approach to life usually create chaos in his underwater world.

The Amazing World of Gumball - 2011-2019

This has to be one of my all time favorite shows. It is family friendly and very funny. Each episode has ita own plot and the mash up of various different types of art styles make it more colorful and appealing to watch.


Summary from imdb:
Welcome to Elmore. This quaint little suburb isn't quite what it seems, as in Elmore, just about everything has the ability to come to life. The Amazing World of Gumball follows the misadventures of Gumball, 12-year-old cat. With help from best friend Darwin, his former pet goldfish that grew legs and joined the family, he gets into all sorts of antics. He is joined by his sister Anais, a four-year-old bunny with heightened intelligence. His parents, Richard and Nicole, provide him support as well.

The Promised Neverland Season 1 - 2019-2021

I do not recommend watching the 2nd season of this show, but the 1st season is a must watch. It has great pasing and you can feel the tension building until it explodes. This show will keep you wanting more and there is constant twists and turns that will always keep you on the edge of your seat.


Summary from imdb:
At Grace Field House, life couldn't be better for the orphans. Though they have no parents, together with the other kids and a kind "Mama" who cares for them, they form one big, happy family. No child is ever overlooked, especially since they are all adopted by the age of 12. Their daily lives involve rigorous tests, but afterwards, they are allowed to play outside. There is only one rule they must obey: do not leave the orphanage. But one day, two top-scoring orphans, Emma and Norman, venture past the gate and unearth the harrowing secret behind their entire existence. Utilizing their quick-wittedness, the children must work together to somehow change their predetermined fate.

The Untamed - 2019

This is the first time that I have ever watched a chinese drama, and I have to say that I enjoyed this one a lot. The settings were breath taking and the story is based around a mystery with a shoking reveal.


Summary from imdb:
Sixteen years ago, the cultivation world was ruled over by five clans: Gusu Lan, Yunmeng Jiang, Qinghe Nie, Qishan Wen, and Lanling Jin. The carefree Wei Wuxian becomes fast friends with the righteous Lan Wangji, and during their adventures, the pair find out that the head of the Wen clan is the evil mastermind behind a series of plots that would wreak havoc upon the lands. Wei Wuxian's attempts to protect the innocent members of the Wen clan from unfair persecution leads to disaster, and he passes away in the process. Wei Wuxian reappears sixteen years later, and works together with good friend Lan Wangji to solve a series of murder mysteries, eventually finding and defeating the true culprit.

The Wallflower - 2006-2007

This show is a bit on the older side, so it does have some outdated views on gender roles, but if you can get past that, this anime is very funny and fun to watch. This show doesn't take itself very seriously which only makes it more enjoyable.


Summary from imdb:
Ever since her crush rejected her by insulting her appearance, Sunako Nakahara has been a shut-in with a hatred for beauty, embracing all things morbid and occult-related. She is sent to live in her aunt's mansion which, to her dismay, she'll share with four exceedingly handsome boys her age. Furthermore, her flighty aunt has made a deal with these boys that in exchange for living there rent-free, they are to turn Sunako into a proper lady by the time she returns from a trip around the world. Thus begins Sunako's hectic life with abrasive Kyohei Takano, ladies' man Ranmaru Morii, calm Takenaga Oda, and friendly Yukinojo Toyama. As she interacts with them she finds them less obnoxious, and she may not be as much of an outcast as she thought.

What We Do In The Shadows - 2019-2024

This show is inspired by the movie of the same name. The documentary style only adds to the comedy of this show. It is a chill watch and I highly recommend.


Summary from imdb:
A look into the nightly lives of four vampires who have lived together on Staten Island for over a century.

Be Yourself - 2021

This is a show about four college students finding themselves and slowly maturing. It's a chill watch.


Summary from imdb:
Four young women begin life at university. Although they are firm friends, they are very different in almost every regard. Xia Lang Lang (Shen Yue) is tomboyish, obstinate, and supremely logical. But as she begins her tertiary educational journey, she discovers that she also has a softer side - and that romance might also play a role in her life. Meanwhile, Huang Fu Shu Min (Zhang Ruo Nan) is always the heroine of her own story. Acting cute and dependent is her strong suit, and independence is not something she has ever been familiar with. But she soon understands that her university journey could well lead her on the path to independence. Then there's Fan Xiao Yu (Vivienne Tien) - an intelligent young woman and a renowned beauty from a wealthy family. But she has always toed the line, respecting her family's wishes, despite the fact that deep down, she wants to break free. When she begins to explore the world of business with a fellow student, she realizes that she might yet attain freedom - and maybe romance, too. Rounding off the quartet is Yang Jia Qian (Zhang Xin Yi), who always gives off the impression of being mature beyond her years. But she has a range of hidden thoughts and feelings that are now ready to come to the fore now she is no longer under the watchful eye of her family.
